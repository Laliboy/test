OUR STORY

Gamers Creating For Gamers

Nexus Studios was founded in 2019 by a group of friends who met through Roblox. We started as players, but we quickly realized we wanted to create our own experiences.

What began as a passion project has grown into a full-fledged studio with over 15 million visits across our games. But we've never lost sight of our roots - we're still gamers at heart, and we build the kinds of games we want to play.

Our philosophy is simple: create immersive worlds, prioritize player enjoyment over monetization, and always listen to community feedback.
